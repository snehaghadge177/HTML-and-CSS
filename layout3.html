This is Article 1 about Kapil Dev

Former captain of the Indian cricket team, he has not only won the world cup back in 1983 but has won a lot of hearts. He was also the coach of the team for October 1999 and August 2000. Kapil Dev has a record in his name for the greatest number of Wickets taken and he retired in the year 1994.
This is Article 2 about M.S. Dhoni.

MS Dhoni who is also known as Mahi is an Indian cricketer who captained the Indian national team from 2007 to 2016 and in the format of Test from 2008 to 2014. Right handed middle order batsman and a great wicket keeper. Widely famous for his shot - helicopter and he is also considered as one of the best finishers.
